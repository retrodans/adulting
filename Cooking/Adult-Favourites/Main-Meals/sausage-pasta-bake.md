# Sausage pasta bake

- approx. duration 40 mins (incl. prep)

## Ingredients

- 1 pack of Sausages chopped
- Mushroom
- Pasta (~300g)
- Cheese
- 1 jar of Carbonara sauce

## Method

- Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan
- Cook sausages for ??? minutes
- Cook pasta
- Mix sausage, pasta, mushrooms
- Bake for 10 minutes
- Stir and cover with cheese
- Bake for 10 minutes
- Serve
